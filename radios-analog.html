What Are Analog Two-Way Radios?

How Analog Radios Work

Analog systems modulate voice signals directly into a radio frequency wave, which is then decoded by the receiving radio. There‚Äôs no digital encoding, so training, programming, and operation tend to be more intuitive and straightforward.
